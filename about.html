My passion for building was ignited as a young child, from playing with Lego to crafiting paper laptops with full keyboard and screen.

During my teenage years, I played with the idea of pursuing Computer Science, but societal opinions labeling it "too masculine" and the myth that it required extraordinary math skills deterred me.

The idea lingered in the back of my mind, but was overshadowed by my career in the Media industry. However, the spark of interest never faded, and in the summer of 2021, I decided to dive into coding through SheCodes, an experience I absolutely loved! 😍

I went on to explore Front-end Development and completed an eight months long part-time certificate course.

I'm passionate about Front-end Development and excited to learn more about React, HTML, CSS and Responsive webdesign.
